## Key Metrics

### OEE Framework
- Availability: Percentage of time equipment is available when needed
- Performance: Speed at which equipment operates compared to ideal
- Quality: Percentage of good units produced

### MOE Framework

- Preventive Maintenance Compliance
- Downtime Reason Categorization
